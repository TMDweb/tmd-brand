    • Data: #33716b or #92112d
  → Light Mode Slides
    • Background: #ffffff
    • Text: #0C0E1D
    • Primary Elements: #29366c
    • Accents: #fcbf2c
    • Data: #92112d or #33716b

[ 4. Header Architecture (Mandatory Layouts) ]
  → Page Number (Global)
    • Placement: Top Left
    • Size: Small
    • Color: #f0f2f9 (Dark Mode) / #2f3e75 (Light Mode)

  → Case 1: Main Title Only
    • Main Title: Top Right / Large, Bold / #ffffff (Dark Mode) or #0C0E1D (Light Mode)
    ⛔ No additional text (No Breadcrumbs, No Subtitles)
    ⛔ No horizontal divider lines under the title

  → Case 2: Main Title + Subtitle
    • Main Title: Top Right / Large, Bold / #ffffff (Dark Mode) or #0C0E1D (Light Mode)
    • Subtitle: Directly below Main Title / Small / #fcbf2c (Dark Mode) or #2f3e75 (Light Mode)
    ⛔ No horizontal divider lines between or under the titles

  → Case 3: Breadcrumb + Main Title
    • Breadcrumb: Above Main Title (Top Right) / Small / #fcbf2c (Dark) or #2f3e75 (Light)
    • Format: [Section Name]  [Subcategory Name]
      ✅ Permitted Separators: Direct text spacing or a vertical pipe |
      ⛔ Prohibited Separators: Do not use arrows like ←
    • Main Title: Directly below Breadcrumb / Large, Bold / #ffffff (Dark) or #0C0E1D (Light)
    ⛔ No horizontal divider lines under the title

  → Footnote (If applicable)
    • Placement: Bottom Right
    • Size: Extra Small
    • Color: #2f3e75

[ 6. Internal Slide Design Elements ]
  → Stat Cards
    ⛔ Solid backgrounds on cards
    ✅ Numbers must float directly on the slide background
    ✅ Separator: Thin vertical colored line on the side of each card
      • #92112d for Engagement and negative sentiment
      • #33716b for Views and ratings
      • #fcbf2c for Publishing, reach, and key metrics
      • If Light Mode: #29366C for Official titles and main pillars
      • If Dark Mode: #0C0E1D for Official titles and main pillars

  → Analytical Text Boxes
    ⛔ Heavy or highly visible backgrounds
    ✅ Extremely subtle background (one shade deeper than slide background)
    ✅ Thin accent line on one side only (Color: #2f3e75 or #29366c)

  → Tables
    • Dark Mode
      → Header: Transparent / Text: Bold Accent Gold #FCBF2C
      → Content Rows: Transparent / Text: White
      → Borders: Thick horizontal Gold line (#FCBF2C) under header / Thin vertical Gold lines / Thin horizontal Gold lines
    • Light Mode
      → Header: Solid Fill #29366c / Text: White
      → Content Rows: Alternating colors (#ffffff and #f0f2f9) / Text: #0C0E1D

  → Comparison Cards
    • Dark Mode: Thin borders #2f3e75 / No background
    • Light Mode: Thin borders #f0f2f9 / Background #f0f2f9

  → Badges & Highlights
    • Background: #fcbf2c / Text: #0C0E1D

  → Arrows & Visual Dividers
    • Colors: #29366c or #fcbf2c
