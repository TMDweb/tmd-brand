The Tradeoff, Honestly
Where embedded wins, and where it does not

Pros

  • One team accountable for the whole funnel, not three vendors.
  • Faster turnaround on briefs, because we sit beside you.
  • Shared dashboards mean shared evidence, not shared opinions.
  • A planned exit means a real handover, not a pricing renewal.

Cons

  • Onboarding takes four to six weeks before output is steady.
  • Headcount appears on a partner invoice before it appears on yours.
  • Tooling is consolidated, which can disrupt legacy contracts.
  • Internal stakeholders need to be available, not just informed.
